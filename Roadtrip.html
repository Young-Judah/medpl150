The pet shop door creaks open. The usual warmth is gone. Empty cages line the walls. A water bowl is still gently moving like something left in a hurry. You and your mom stand outside the pet shop. The wind feels colder now. A car is parked nearby. The map is unfolding in your mom’s hands.

“…Mom?” Your voice echoes more than it should. Your mom slowly walks forward.

“This… isn’t right.” Your mom says unsteadily

A shop worker rushes in from the back, breathing heavily.

The worker says panicked, “You shouldn’t be here. They’re gone.”

So then your mom responds with, "Gone? what do you mean gone?"

The worker points to the empty cages

"All the animals. Someone came in last night and took them. Transported them out of state." The worker said shakily.

You look at your mom with big round eyes full of adventure!

You ask, "Well shouldn't we go after the sweet lil animals?!"
