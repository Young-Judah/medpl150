For this assignment I wanted to touch on a subject I do not normally explore in my daily life. I am aware of the existence of my father. That statement may sound very distant but I have become comfortable having that part of my life mysterious so to speak. In a way the absence of my father gives me more and more motivation to explore a whole new type of culture that is so unorthodox from our very New York roots. This assignment very much interested me because it allows me to explore the way in which I mostly view my relationship with my father and the culture that comes with his side of the family.
